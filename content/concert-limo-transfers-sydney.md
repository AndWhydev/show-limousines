## Frequently Asked Questions
#### Can we play our own music in the limo?
Absolutely! All our limousines come with Bluetooth and high-quality speakers. Your friendly driver will help you to connect your phone to the sound system so that you can play your own music and enjoy the ride.
#### Can we make more than one stop?
Of course. We are happy to plan scheduled stops and pick up other guests on the way to your destination. Please make sure you inform us of any additional stops in advance so your driver can plan the best route and ensure you get to the venue on time.
#### What areas do you service?
We offer limousine hire and transfers across Sydney, Wollongong, Campbelltown, Marrickville, and Parramatta.
#### What other services do you provide?
We can provide limo services for a wide range of occasions. These include weddings, school formals, birthday parties, airport transfers, and funeral car hire.
