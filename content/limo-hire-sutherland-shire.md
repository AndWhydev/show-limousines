## FAQs
#### Where can I go with a limo hire?
Our luxury limos provide pickup and drop-off anywhere in the Sutherland Shire – Cronulla, Miranda, Caringbah, Gymea, Sutherland, Engadine, Menai and can take you anywhere within Sydney. Your assigned chauffeur will arrive on time and drive you safely based on your itinerary.
#### How many people can be seated in the limo?
We have a range of limousines with various seating capacities of up to 16 passengers, such as the Hummer Stretch limo. We also have limos with more private seating such as the Rolls-Royce or Mercedes S Class sedans, which comfortably seat 4 passengers.
#### Are drinks included in a limo hire?
We cannot supply alcoholic drinks due to regulations – however, we can arrange for a BYO policy and in-vehicle coolers and glasses so that you can enjoy your favourite drinks on the journey.
