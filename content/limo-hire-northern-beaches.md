## Frequently Asked Questions
### Is there a BYO policy?
Yes, we can arrange for you to bring your favourite drinks on board to enjoy during your transit. However, we are unable to serve alcohol due to regulations.
### How early in advance do I need to book a limo hire?
Demand for our limousines is high during peak holiday periods (November-December) – we recommend booking at least 2 weeks before the event to secure your chosen vehicle and timing.
### Can I arrange for pick-up and drop-off at multiple locations?
Yes. Your chauffeur can take you and your party anywhere within the Northern Beaches and across Sydney. We will advise if certain areas are inaccessible due to the size of our limos.
