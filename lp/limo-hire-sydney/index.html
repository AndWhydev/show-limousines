FAQs

Common questions

Do you offer wedding cars in Sydney?

Yes — Rolls Royce Phantom, Chrysler Super Stretch, Hummer Stretch, Mercedes S Class AMG and Mercedes Sprinter Limo Vans, all across Sydney.

How much does it cost to hire a limousine?

Pricing varies by vehicle, location, and hours. Get in touch for an exact quote.

What's included when I book?

Uniformed chauffeur, bottled water, air conditioning, Bluetooth. For weddings: red carpet for the bride, satin ribbon, sparkling wine.

How far in advance do I need to book?

As early as possible — weddings are often booked 12 months ahead. Last-minute bookings also welcome.

What areas do you service?

Greater Sydney and Wollongong, including Eastern Suburbs, Inner West, Parramatta, Penrith, Campbelltown, Sutherland Shire, Northern Beaches, and the Illawarra.
