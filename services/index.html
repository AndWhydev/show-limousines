FAQs

Common questions, answered.

How much does it cost to hire a limousine?

The cost to hire a limo will vary depending on the vehicle, location, and pick up/drop off requirements. Most limousine hire companies will charge by the hour. At Show Limousines, all of our limousine hire services start at $399 per booking. For a more accurate quote, get in touch with our team and we'll send it over via email.

What services do you offer?

We cover every occasion across Sydney and Wollongong — weddings, school formals, birthdays, hens and bucks nights, corporate transfers, airport and cruise transfers, concerts, sporting events and funerals. Whatever the event, we have a vehicle and a chauffeur to match. If you're not sure which service fits, get in touch and we'll talk you through the options.

What's included when I book a limo?

For all transfers and events, we include a uniformed chauffeur, bottled water, and a luxurious modern vehicle. Each car is fitted with air conditioning, bluetooth, drink holders, party lights, and comfortable, luxurious seating. For weddings, we also roll out the red carpet for the bride, lace the cars with satin ribbon and provide sparkling wine.

How far in advance do I need to book?

We recommend booking as far in advance as possible, particularly for an event or special occasion, as we want to ensure that you get the right vehicle and can secure the date. For peak dates like formals and weddings, we take bookings months — even a year — ahead. That being said, we do take on last-minute bookings and will always do our best to accommodate requests.

How many people can fit into a limo?

That depends on the vehicle chosen! For our stretch limousines and hummer limousines, we can fit up to 16 guests in one car. For smaller parties, we can seat 4 to 8 passengers, and our minibuses and limo vans carry larger groups. For bigger events, we offer packages where you can hire multiple cars for the one occasion.

What areas do you service?

We service the entirety of Sydney – from Western Sydney, to the Northern Beaches, all the way down to the Sutherland Shire and Wollongong. We regularly chauffeur our clients from Sydney’s CBD to Parramatta, Campbelltown, the Eastern Suburbs, Marrickville and the Inner West and everywhere in between.
