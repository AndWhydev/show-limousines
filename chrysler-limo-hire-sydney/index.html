Sydney Chrysler Super Stretch Limousines For Hire

Travel in ultimate luxury with our Chrysler limousines in Sydney. Whether it’s for a wedding, hen’s night, school formal, or private party, we can arrange for a VIP transportation experience with the Show Limousines exclusive range of super stretch and gullwing fleet of Chryslers.

Chrysler Super Stretch Limousines are renowned for having a sleek, stylish veneer that exudes opulence. With fleet options in both white and black, our Chrysler limos are equipped with lush seating interiors, state-of-the-art sound and lighting, and a refreshment bar for champagne.

The Show Limousines Chrysler Super Stretch limousines are perfect for weddings and are available for hire anywhere across Sydney. Seating up to 10 passengers, our Chrysler Super Stretch limos provide the best in luxury wedding transportation for the bridal party. Each booking comes with a uniformed chauffeur, a red carpet rollout upon arrival, and complimentary drinks on board. Following the ceremony, our driver remains available for your getaway services, ensuring that the newlyweds get to their reception on time.

Book your Chrysler limousine hire in Sydney in advance to secure your timings and dates – contact using our enquiry form or call 0422 023 413.

Weddings

Our Black Edition Chrysler Super Stretch limousine is the perfect way to arrive at your ceremony in style. Whether you're tying the knot at the iconic Sergeants Mess in Mosman, exchanging vows at a garden estate in the Blue Mountains, or celebrating at a waterfront venue like Doltone House Jones Bay Wharf, we've got you covered. With a dedicated fifth door designed for brides and bridal parties, stepping out in your gown has never been more graceful. We coordinate pickups across all Sydney suburbs, transporting the bride's party from home to ceremony, and the newlyweds onward to their reception, seamlessly and on time.

Birthdays and Hens Nights

Sydney's best nights out deserve the right entrance. Our Chrysler Super Stretch fits up to 10 passengers, making it ideal for a hens night crawl through Darlinghurst and Kings Cross, a glamorous dinner at Cockle Bay Wharf, or a night of cocktails in Newtown or Surry Hills. We pick up from anywhere across Sydney, so whether your group is spread across the Northern Beaches or the Inner West, we'll bring everyone together before the night begins.

School Formal

Add a touch of elegance to the Year 12 formal with a limo hire. Our Chrysler Super Stretch handles multiple pickups across Sydney suburbs, from Castle Hill to Cronulla, before delivering your group to venues like Waterview in Bicentennial Park or the Novotel Sydney on Darling Harbour. Up to 10 passengers can travel together, allowing you and your friends to experience true luxury before kicking off the celebration.
